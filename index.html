PRIVACY POLICY – FINTRACK

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your email via Google Login for authentication.
  • User-Entered Data: We store transactions you enter, including amount, category, time, and personal notes.
  • Device Data and Logs: Includes device type, operating system, IP address, and interaction data to ensure security and improve app performance. This data may be automatically collected via Google Firebase.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Display spending reports and statistics.
  • Manage budgets and send alerts.
  • Provide AI-powered analysis when you use relevant features.
  • Detect errors, prevent unauthorized access, and ensure system security.
  • Improve user experience and app performance.

3. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data.

Your data may be processed or stored by the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, data storage, analytics)
  • Google Cloud Platform (cloud infrastructure)

These services are used solely to provide and operate the application. We may provide data when legally required by authorities in compliance with applicable laws.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is encrypted and stored on Google Cloud's secure infrastructure.
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

Your data will be stored:

  • Until you delete your account, or
  • When no longer necessary for service purposes, or
  • As required by law.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, view, and edit your personal data directly within the app.
  • Export all transaction data to a CSV file.
  • Permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time via the settings menu.
  • Request support to access, edit, or delete your data via the contact email.

6. Tracking Technologies

We may use analytics tools such as Firebase Analytics to collect usage data to improve services.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 years old, and we do not knowingly collect data from this age group.
