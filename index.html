PRIVACY POLICY – FINTRACK

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your email address through Google Login for authentication.
  • User-entered data: Transactions you manually enter, including amount, category, time, personal notes, and budgets.
  • Receipt images: When you use the receipt capture or upload feature, the app may process images to recognize receipt content and assist with expense entry.
  • Voice data: When you use the voice expense entry feature, the app may process audio to convert speech into text.
  • Device and log data: This may include device type, operating system, IP address, and interaction data to ensure security, detect errors, and improve app performance. This data may be collected automatically through Google Firebase.
  • Payment information: We do not directly store your card details or payment methods. In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We may only receive information related to your subscription status to provide the appropriate service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Display personal expense reports and statistics.
  • Manage budgets and provide alerts.
  • Recognize receipt content, such as amount, transaction date, and expense category.
  • Convert speech into text to support quick expense entry.
  • Provide intelligent analysis when you use AI-related features.
  • Authenticate accounts and manage service usage status.
  • Detect errors, prevent unauthorized access, and ensure system security.
  • Improve the app experience and performance.

3. Receipt Image Processing

FinTrack allows users to capture or upload receipt images to support expense recording and analysis.

Receipt images may be automatically processed using text recognition technology to extract information such as amount, transaction date, store name, and expense category.

We only use image data to provide app functionality and do not use receipt images for advertising purposes.

4. Voice Data Processing

FinTrack may use microphone permission to support voice-based expense entry.

Voice data may be processed to convert speech into text. The converted content may be used to create expense transactions based on your request.

We do not use voice data for purposes other than providing voice input functionality, unless otherwise notified or permitted by you.

5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data.

Your data may be processed or stored by the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: authentication, data storage, analytics, and crash/error reporting.
  • Google Cloud Platform: data storage and processing infrastructure.
  • Apple App Store or Google Play: payment processing and subscription management.
  • AI, OCR, or speech-to-text processing services, if used by the app to provide receipt recognition or voice input features.

These services are used only to provide, operate, and improve the app. We may disclose data when legally required by competent authorities under applicable law.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on Google Cloud’s secure systems.
  • We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.
  • Payment information such as card numbers, bank accounts, or payment methods is not stored directly by FinTrack.

Your data will be retained:

  • Until you delete your account, or
  • When it is no longer necessary for providing the service, or
  • As required by law.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, view, and edit your personal data directly in the app.
  • Export all transaction data to a CSV file if this feature is supported.
  • Permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time in Settings.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription through the App Store or Google Play.
  • Request support to access, correct, or delete your data via the contact email.

8. Tracking Technologies

We may use analytics tools such as Firebase Analytics to collect usage data to improve our service.

9. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If there are important changes, we will update the content on this page or notify you in the app when necessary.
